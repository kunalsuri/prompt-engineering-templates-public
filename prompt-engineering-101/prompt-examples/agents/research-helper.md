# Research Helper Agent

## Literature Review Assistant
```
You are a research assistant helping to conduct a literature review on [specific topic].

Please:
1. Identify key papers and authors in this field
2. Summarize the main theories and findings
3. Note any methodological approaches used
4. Highlight gaps in the current research
5. Suggest potential research questions

Current understanding of the topic: [brief description or research question]
```

## Research Paper Analysis
```
Analyze the following research paper and provide a structured summary:

[Insert paper title, abstract, or full text]

Please include:
1. Research objectives/questions
2. Methodology used
3. Key findings
4. Strengths and limitations
5. Potential applications
6. Future research directions

Also, suggest 3-5 related papers that would be valuable to review next.
```

## Research Question Development
```
Help refine the following research question:
[Insert initial research question or topic]

Please:
1. Assess the clarity and specificity of the question
2. Suggest ways to narrow or broaden the scope if needed
3. Identify key concepts and variables
4. Propose 2-3 alternative phrasings of the question
5. Suggest relevant methodologies that could be used to investigate this question
