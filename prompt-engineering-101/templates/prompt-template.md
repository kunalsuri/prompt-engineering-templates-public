# Prompt Template

## Basic Structure
```
[Role/Expertise]
You are [describe role, expertise, or perspective].

[Context]
[Provide necessary background information]

[Task]
[Clearly state what you want the AI to do]

[Instructions]
1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

[Constraints/Requirements]
- [Specific requirement 1]
- [Specific requirement 2]
- [Specific requirement 3]

[Output Format]
[Specify the desired format or structure of the response]
```

## Advanced Template with Optional Sections
```
# [PROMPT TITLE]

## Role & Expertise
[Describe the AI's role and any specific expertise]

## Objective
[State the primary goal of this prompt]

## Context
[Provide background information, including:
- Relevant details about the subject matter
- Any constraints or limitations
- Previous related work or decisions]

## Task
[Clearly define what you want the AI to do, including:
- Specific actions to take
- Questions to answer
- Problems to solve]

## Input Data
[Provide any necessary input data, examples, or references]

## Constraints & Requirements
[List any specific requirements or constraints, such as:
- Formatting requirements
- Length limitations
- Style guidelines
- Required sections or elements]

## Output Format
[Specify exactly how the output should be structured, including:
- Required sections
- Headings and subheadings
- Any specific formatting (bullets, tables, etc.)
- Length expectations]

## Examples
[Include 1-2 examples of good outputs]

## Additional Notes
[Any other relevant information or special instructions]
